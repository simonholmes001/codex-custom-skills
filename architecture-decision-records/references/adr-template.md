# ADR Template

## Title

Short decision name.

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated

## Context

Problem, constraints, and assumptions driving this decision.

## Decision

Chosen option and precise scope.

## Alternatives Considered

List options with brief rationale for rejection.

## Consequences

- Positive outcomes
- Negative tradeoffs
- Risks and mitigations

## Implementation Plan

Phased actions, owner, and sequencing.

## Validation

How success/failure will be measured.

## Review Trigger

Date or condition that should trigger reassessment.
