# Design Areas Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure design completeness.

## Enterprise Enrollment and Organization

- Tenant strategy and enterprise agreement/MCA context.
- Management group hierarchy and subscription lifecycle.

## Identity and Access

- Entra tenant boundaries and B2B/B2C needs.
- RBAC model, PIM/JIT, break-glass strategy, access reviews.

## Network Topology and Connectivity

- Hub-spoke vs Virtual WAN decision.
- Address planning, overlap avoidance, private access model.
- On-prem/partner connectivity and egress inspection strategy.

## Security and Governance

- Policy initiatives, exemptions, and drift handling.
- Defender/SIEM integration and baseline hardening posture.

## Management and Operations

- Centralized monitoring, alerting, and retention controls.
- Backup/DR model mapped to workload tiers.
- Incident/change runbooks and ownership model.

## Platform Automation

- Subscription vending and bootstrap automation.
- IaC standards and CI/CD policy gates.
